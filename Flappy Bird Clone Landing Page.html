Game Overview

Flappy Bird is the legendary arcade game that took the world by storm. Your mission is deceptively simple: guide a pixelated bird through an endless series of green pipes by tapping or clicking. Each successful passage increases your score, but one wrong move means instant game over. Its minimalist design and punishing difficulty created a global phenomenon that captured millions of players' hearts.
