Survival Games

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating survival game that tests your ability to withstand waves of increasingly challenging monsters. With simple controls but deep strategy, this game offers hours of entertainment as you fight to survive as long as possible.

How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience points to level up. Choose new abilities and upgrades as you progress to become stronger and face the mounting monster threat.

Game Features

Endless Action

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in an endless survival challenge.

Character Progression

Level up your character and unlock powerful abilities to combat stronger enemies.

Various Enemies

Battle different types of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Power-ups & Items

Collect power-ups and items to enhance your character and survive longer.

Strategy Tips

Success in Monster Survivors requires careful planning and quick reflexes. Focus on movement to avoid being surrounded, prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle, and remember that sometimes retreating is the best option to regroup and attack from a better position.

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors draws inspiration from popular roguelike games while adding its own unique twist. The game balances accessibility for new players with depth for experienced gamers, creating an engaging experience that keeps you coming back for "just one more run".

Zombie Crusher OOZ

Zombie Crusher OOZ is an action-packed zombie killing game where you must survive against hordes of the undead. Take control of powerful weapons and vehicles to crush, shoot, and destroy zombies in this adrenaline-fueled adventure.

Game Features

Zombie Crushing Action

Destroy zombies with a variety of weapons and vehicles in fast-paced combat scenarios.

Arsenal Upgrades

Unlock and upgrade powerful weapons to deal with increasingly difficult zombie swarms.

Challenging Levels

Face progressively harder challenges as you advance through the game's levels and environments.

Zombie Varieties

Battle different types of zombies, each with unique abilities and weaknesses to exploit.

Strategy Tips

Prioritize targets based on threat level, conserve ammunition when possible, and utilize environmental advantages. Learn each zombie type's weaknesses and adapt your tactics accordingly for maximum efficiency in eliminating the undead threat.

About Zombie Crusher OOZ

Zombie Crusher OOZ combines classic zombie survival elements with fast-paced action gameplay. Its intuitive controls and addictive gameplay make it accessible to casual gamers while offering enough depth and challenge to satisfy experienced players looking for intense zombie-slaying action.

Game Features

Stronghold Building

Epic Adventure

Hazmob FPS Online Shooter

Hazmob FPS Online Shooter is an intense first-person shooter game that puts your combat skills to the test in fast-paced multiplayer battles. Engage in thrilling firefights across various maps with different weapons and tactical options.

Game Features

Online Multiplayer

Compete against players from around the world in various game modes and maps.

Weapon Arsenal

Choose from a diverse selection of firearms, each with unique handling, damage, and tactical advantages.

Tactical Gameplay

Employ strategic positioning, map awareness, and resource management to outmaneuver opponents.

Character Customization

Personalize your shooter with various skins, equipment, and loadout options as you progress.

Strategy Tips

Learn the maps and common player routes to gain a positional advantage. Practice weapon recoil control for more accurate shots. When low on health, don't hesitate to retreat and recuperate before re-engaging. Communication with teammates is crucial in team-based modes.

Whether you're a casual player looking for quick action or a competitive gamer seeking to test your skills, this game offers an immersive shooting experience that will keep you coming back for more.

Game Features

Random Challenges

Face unpredictable obstacles and situations that change with each game session.

Unique Environments

Play basketball in a variety of whimsical settings, each with their own physics and challenges.

Progressive Difficulty

Encounter increasingly challenging levels that test your shooting skills and adaptability.

Fun Physics

Experience entertaining and sometimes hilarious physics-based gameplay that defies conventional basketball rules.

Observe each level carefully before shooting to identify potential obstacles and opportunities. Practice your timing and power control to master different shooting scenarios. Don't get frustrated by the random elements—adaptability is part of the fun! Sometimes a seemingly impossible shot can lead to unexpected success.

About Basket Random

Basket Random puts a delightful twist on traditional basketball games by introducing unpredictable elements that make every shot an adventure. Perfect for casual players looking for something different, this game combines skill-based gameplay with random challenges that create moments of both frustration and triumph. Its lighthearted approach to sports gaming ensures an entertaining experience that doesn't take itself too seriously.
